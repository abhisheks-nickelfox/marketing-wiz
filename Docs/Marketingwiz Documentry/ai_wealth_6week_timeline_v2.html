Upcoming

  • 100-user table — Name + avatar, Total Tasks (clickable count), Pulse availability badge

  • Permission-based UI enforcement — hide/disable elements by role + extra permissions
  • Responsive layout check across all 6 weeks of screens
  • Accessibility pass — keyboard nav, focus rings, aria labels

Team Pulse is the lightest Figma module — primarily a read view. The bulk of Week 6 is cross-feature data wiring, real API integration, and QA across all 35+ screens delivered in Weeks 1–5.

iDisclaimer — all timelines are subject to change

All timelines are estimates based on the Figma designs reviewed in April 2026 and are subject to change without notice. Timelines may be revised if: (1) Figma designs are updated, revised or expanded — any redesign of a delivered screen restarts development effort for that screen; (2) new features are added to the Figma scope mid-sprint; (3) technical dependencies, API design decisions or third-party integrations are unresolved ahead of the relevant week; (4) QA feedback requires significant rework; or (5) team capacity changes. The extended timeline (Weeks 7–10) is entirely contingent on missing features being designed in Figma before development begins — no development will be started on undesigned features. This document is a living roadmap and should be reviewed and updated after every Figma revision.
